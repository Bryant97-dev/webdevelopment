DRAWING

Drawing is my passion since I'M kid. I like drawing anime, semi realism, realism, and so many things . Now I usually draw with my pen tablet since first January this year. Pen tablet is tool for digital drawing, before that is usually drawing with traditional tool like mostly pencil, drawing pen and pencil color and of course paper for drawing XD. When drawing Digital I usually use program called Clip Studio Paint and sometimes Photoshop. I like Clip Studio Paint because it doesn't need so many space file drawing like Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint file can be use too on Photoshop and Clip Studio Paint have good environment for artist so we can do work effectively, like we can download brush from artist and can be transferred automatically to Clip Studio Paint program.

SIMPLE COLORING TIPS

Base

First, add base color for your artwork. My little tips is to choose base color you must choose color that not too dark or bright so when you want to make highlight or shadow you have more range color to choose.

Shading/Shadow

Second, add shadow to the place that mostly will not directly by light. My tips so must have imagination where the highlight is come from so you has clear vision where is the place will be the shadow part. In this step you need focus to have clear vision where is the shadow part will be look like so you can ignore the detail part first.

Rendering

Final, in this step you just need add your detail part to your drawing. Most common part on rendering are reflective light, smoothing shading, add highlight, blending color with background. But the most important is find your style to translete your aesthetic and fundamental view to your artwork.
